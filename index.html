Intro

Hi! My name is Angelica Michaca (she/her/hers) and I am a computer science major from Barnard College of Columbia University. As a first-generation Latina student I am set on paving the way for other students of minority heritages in higher education as well as in the tech field by mobilizing diverse engineers into tech and promoting the #iLookLikeAnEngineer movement. I am currently working on projects and am applying to full time roles as a software engineer or similar tech roles. I am influenced by all creative and immersive facets of technology and my work demonstrates a great understanding of web development, front-end,and back-end! While my language of choice is Python, I am also proficient in Java, C, Javascript, HTML, SQL, and RubyonRails which im currently learning.
