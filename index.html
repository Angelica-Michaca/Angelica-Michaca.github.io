Intro

Hi! My name is Angelica Michaca (she/her/hers) and I am a junior at Barnard College of Columbia University studying Computer Science. As a first-generation Latina student who is set to graduate from Columbia University in December '22, I am set on paving the way for latinx excellence in higher education as well as in the tech field by making space for myself and others who identify as software engineers of color. I am currently working on projects and am applying to internships and professional positions. I am influenced by all creative and immersive facets of technology and my work demonstrates a great understanding of web development, front-end, back-end, and an interest in cyber security! While my language of choice is Python, I am proficient in Java, HTML, CSS, C, and vim, as well as the design tool, Figma.

Work

What am I up to? This semester I am taking Computer Science Theory and Fundamentals of Computer Systems. Thanks to my Fundamentals of Computer Systems course, understanding and computing digital logic has sparked my interest in the sequential and combinational logic used in circuitry! In addition to school, I am working on personal projects and implementing the tools and knowledge from my computer science courses at Columbia University. Lastly, I have been spending a moderate amount of my time practicing and solving challenging leetcode problems. Overall, I am an ambitous learner who enjoys taking courses outside the scope of what is taught at my University and recieving certificates to add to my resume and skillset.
